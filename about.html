Background

Who I am

Two decades in technology consulting, executing engagements inside multiple Fortune 500 organizations while handling all aspects of a modern data & analytics practice at a Tier 1 consulting firm. The connective tissue across all of it has been deep, hands-on technical work — modeling, pipelines, cloud warehouses, dbt — paired with the kind of direct executive communication that is usually walled off in larger consulting engagements.

Dorian Insights is the practice around this work today: a senior-principal consultancy focused on data and analytics, business strategy, and the technology architecture that supports them. Most current engagements involve direct platform work — across cloud data warehouses (e.g. Snowflake, BigQuery), dbt, and the broader modern data stack — typically in service of a strategic question that started with leadership.

Current focus areas include building and operating cloud data platforms; team and process design; integration of LLMs and AI into analytical workflows; and full-stack analysis from raw data through executive presentation.
